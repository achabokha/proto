Account and card verification

What documents are required for my proof of residency?

You may provide any official document that bears both your name and your current address. It can be a utility bill, a bank statement or a residency certificate.

What are the card limits, restrictions and fees?

As part of our AML policy there are certain restriction and limitations in place. There is no limit for on-line purchases, however, there is a daily limit of ATM withdrawals, set at 1,500.00 EUR/USD.

What is KYC and AML?

The KYC and AML, which are abbreviations of “Know Your Customer” and “Anti-Money Laundering” respectively, are transparency policies that every financial services provider must comply with in order to protect themselves and their customers.

What documents are required for proof of identity?

You may provide any government issued ID that shows your full name in English and a recent photo. It can be a driver’s license, a passport or an ID card. Please make sure that the identity proof document is not expired and shows the entire document. Submissions with partial or expired proof of identity will be rejected.

Help

My card has been stolen/lost!

If you have lost your card or suspect it has been stolen, please contact Customer Support immediately to block your card. A new card will be issued and your account balance (EUR or USD, depending on your card) will be transferred to it.

I misplaced my card, what do I do?

If you are sure you will not be able to find it or you suspect it could be stolen, please contact Customer Support. Your current card will be blocked to avoid unsolicited use and a new card will be issued to you. Your current card balance will be transferred to your new card during this process.

There are problems with my card balance, what do I do?

There are certain fees we charge for various types of transactions.
If you suspect you are being overcharged when using your card please contact our Customer Support immediately and we will investigate.

My transaction was declined. What do i do?

Ensure the payment option you are using supports MasterCard service. If they do accept MasterCard and you still encounter issues making payments with your card, please contact Customer Support.

How can I contact Customer Support?

You can contact Customer Support anytime by click Help button in the right left corner.

Features

Can I have multiple cards linked to my account?

Yes, you are allowed to sign up for as many cards as you wish. Each will have it’s own balance and associated wallet address.

What FIAT currencies are supported?

Euro or U.S. Dollar. We have no plans at this time to support other FIAT currencies.

What digital assets can I use to load my card?

Bitcoin and Litecoin. We are working on adding other digital assets.

Orders & Shipping

How do I order a card?

To order a card, you must sign up for an account first. This account is going to be your card management and overview hub. Once you have created the account and verified your e-mail, you may apply for the card from our Products page. Please note that you may apply for only one card per application. If you wish to have an additional card, please re-submit your application.

I have not received my card.

It usually takes 5 working days for you to receive your card, from submission to delivery. If you have been waiting for your card considerably longer than 5 working days, please contact Customer Support, submit a ticket and we will investigate the issue.

When will I receive my card?

You should receive your card within 5 working days of placing the order.

Can I order a company card?

No, we do not offer our services to companies and juristic persons.

Usage

How do I reset my password?

If you entered wrong password you will see an option to reset your password by clicking on the “Forgot your password?” link. You will receive an e-mail with a link that will allow you to reset your password. If you are not receiving the reset e-mail, make sure service@embily.com is not blocked and that the reset e-mail has not been landing in your Spam box.

How are refunds to card purchases handled?

Fiat transaction refunds, once processed, are returned to the card that made the payment.

Where can I find my card PIN?

Your cards and their PIN codes are shipped in the same package, however the PIN code itself is protected by a peel-proof holographic sticker. If upon receiving your card, you suspect your PIN or card itself has been tampered with, please contact support desk and submit a ticket, we will investigate further. Do not use the suspect card.

How do I check my current balance?

You may do that by logging in the web site.

Can I add my card to my Apple Pay/Samsung Pay wallet?

Yes, it will work since both services accept MasterCard. Any payment service that accepts MasterCard as a payment option will accept your card.

Can I use my card to sign up for subscription-based services?

Yes. As long as the service accepts MasterCard.

How do I transfer funds without web site access?

Your card has an assigned wallet address to it. Just simply send your digital assets to this address. The funds will be automatically processed and deposited to your card. You will receive an email notification.

I just transferred digital assets to my card wallet. How long will it take for the transfer to be completed?

In most cases, it takes 30 minutes. However, in some cases, it might take up to several hours due to blockchain congestion.

Do I need to change my card currency to that of the country I am traveling to?

No, you do not. You will receive and make payments in local currency regardless of currency of your card. Some stores, however, offer to process your purchase in your card currency. Please, consider choosing this method to avoid foreign transaction fees and exchange rate.
