Embily Card

The Card for Digital Assets

Embily Card can be loaded using BTC and LTC. Using Embily Card you can make purchases in stores and online or just withdraw hard cash from an ATM wherever MasterCard is accepted. Once registered with Embily platform you can simply send BTC and LTC to a crypto address assign to your Embily Card without ever need to login to Embily again. That's it!

Card Features

  • Use a crypto wallet of your choice to load Embily Card
  • Skip login to Embily, send your crypto currency to a crypto address assigned to Card
  • Cards are available in USD and EUR
  • Load Card using BTC or LTC
  • Fast Card delivery, get it within 5 business days
  • Experienced customer support
